About SE-Arch

SE-Arch is a security-focused, currently work in progress Linux distribution based on Arch Linux, designed to provide enhanced security features and robust protection for your system.

Key Features

  • Advanced Security Measures
  • MAC Address Randomization
  • Full Disk Encryption
  • Enhanced Kernel Hardening
  • Protection for the Root Account
  • And Much More...
